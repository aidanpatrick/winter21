About debate:


What we do: At SAAS, we do Public Forum debate. Every month, we get a new resolution, which is the topic that we will argue either for or against. Then, we write a case, which is a 4 minute-length "essay" that is either pro (for the resolution) or con (against). Throughout the year, there are debate tournaments where you and a partner will argue with your cases. From then on, it is a cold-blooded battle with potential casualties. May the best debaters win.
